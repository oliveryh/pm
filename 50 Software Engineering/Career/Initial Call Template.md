Stock questions (I normally pick 2 or 3):

Generic:

- What was the best team you've worked in?
- What do you think is the biggest gap in your knowledge of the languages and frameworks you currently use?
- What do you like most about the product you're currently working on?
- What do you do best?

SW:

- How much have you worked in cross-functional teams - i.e. with product managers, designers, etc.?
- When working in a team, how do you decide when to ask for help?
- What's an opinion about software development you hold very strongly?
- Tell me something I might not know about software development

FE:

- What's your preferred approach to testing frontend code?
- What's an opinion about frontend development you hold very strongly?
- What techniques do you use to investigate frontend performance issues?
- What new web technologies or trends are you particularly excited about?
- What do you think the web will look like in 5 years' time?
