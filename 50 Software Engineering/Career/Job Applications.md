# Interviews

## Preparation
- Know the industry, organisation, and role
- Know your CV and have an elevator pitch ready
- Prepare questions to ask

## Competency Based
Use **S.T.A.R** (Situation, Task, Action, Result) with first-hand examples only. Avoid "we" — they're assessing you.

## Wrapping Up
- Thank the interviewer(s)
- Ask the **recruiter** (not interviewer) for feedback afterwards; don't chase

# Cover Letter

~2 paragraphs. Complements (not repeats) your CV — use it to differentiate yourself, explain why you suit the role and chose the industry. Avoid name dropping.

# CV

Keep to 1 page (unless highly technical). No opening statement, DOB, photo, or creative touches.

**Structure:** Contact details → Education → Work Experience → Interests/Achievements

**Formatting:** Conventional typeface, bolded headers, bullet points over long sentences.
