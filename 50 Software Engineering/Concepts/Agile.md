Kaizen (改善, かいぜん), the [Sino-Japanese](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sino-Japanese_vocabulary) word for "improvement", is a concept referring to business activities that [continuously improve](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Continual_improvement_process) all functions and involve all employees from the [CEO](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/CEO) to the [assembly line](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assembly_line) workers.
