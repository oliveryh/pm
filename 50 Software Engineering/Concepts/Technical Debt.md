**Thoughts on Tech Debt from SC**
> Some thoughts about tech debt / ticket-level-FTVS I'm interested in your opinions on:  
> 
> - IMO it's often (even _usually_) valuable to get a very rough version of something merged and into QA (behind a flag) as early as possible, e.g. within the first couple of days of a sprint:
> 
> - QA can start sooner, and thus get feedback when we still have plenty of time to course-correct before the end of the sprint
> - Avoids blocking others who may need to build upon it
> - As with all timeboxing, it encourages us to pause and reflect on what's important before over-committing
> 
> - i.e. fixed-time/variable-scope applied to tickets (not just to sprints or initiatives)
> - This requires liberally accruing debt (tech/product/design) early in the sprint — which of course needs a rigorous plan to pay it down
> - Maybe it's useful to categorise debt by the _timescale_ in which we plan to pay off debt:
> 
> 1. High cost debt: Pay off this sprint
> 2. Medium cost debt: Pay off this initiative
> 3. Low cost debt: Pay off longer term
