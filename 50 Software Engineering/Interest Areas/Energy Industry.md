# Old World

1. Power plant generates electricity
2. Transformer steps up voltage for transmission
3. Transmission step
4. Distribution network

# Key Roles

1.  Supplier (Bulb, Octopus, eon etc.)
2.  Generation
3.  Networks
	1.  Transmission Network Operator - Carry electricity at high voltages for long distances
	2.  Distribution Network Operator - Carry electricity at low voltages for local areas
	3.  Electricity System Operator

## Transmission Network Operator (TNO)

- Maintain physical infrastructure of the grid
- Allow new connections

## Distribution Network Operators (DNO)

- Maintain the physical infrastructure of the network

## Electricity System Operator (ESO)

- Ensure that the amount of electricity being consumed is matched by the amount of energy being generated

# Renewables

- Renewables tend to be smaller, so are largely connected to the distribution network as opposed to the transmission grid
