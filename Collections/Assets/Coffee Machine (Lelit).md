## Descaling

![Cleaning Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8nb2rkKwJuA)

1. Turn off the machine
2. Fill water tank with 1L of water and 100ml of descaling solution
3. Mix the solution
4. Turn on the machine
5. Remove the filter holder and place a container under the grouphead
6. Withdraw water for 30 seconds
7. Wait for 30 seconds
8. Clear up splashes of water with a cloth to avoid damaging the outer machine surface
9. Withdraw water from the steam wand for 30 seconds
10. Wait for 30 seconds
11. Repeat these steps until the task has run out
12. Rinse the water tank and fill it with fresh water
13. Repeat the withdraw operations from the group and steam wand until at least 2 tanks of fresh water are used up (or water runs clear)
