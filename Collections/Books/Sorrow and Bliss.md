# Review

In the book, the author doesn't explicitly mention what the main character is living with. It allowed me to project what my own experience has been with having people to me live with a mental health issue.

Putting a name to what you're feeling was the turning point for Martha. I have spoken to friends about their experiences and it was an important part of their experience too.
