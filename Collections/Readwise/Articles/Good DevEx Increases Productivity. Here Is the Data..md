- Summary: Providing a good developer experience (DevEx) has been shown to increase productivity and innovation, according to new research. Factors such as flow state, cognitive load, and feedback loops were found to have a significant impact on individual, team, and organizational outcomes. By blocking time for deep work, companies can achieve a 50% increase in productivity, while creating intuitive processes leads to a 50% increase in innovation. Fast code turnaround times result in a 20% increase in innovation. The research provides evidence-based outcomes and advice for business leaders to make the case for investing in DevEx.
