## Metadata

- Author: [[Steph Ango]]
- Full Title: File Over App
- Category: #articles
- Summary: "File over app" is a philosophy that emphasizes the importance of creating digital artifacts that last, using files that are easy to control and retrieve. The files you create are more important than the tools used to create them, as they have a chance to last beyond the ephemeral nature of apps. This philosophy appeals to tool makers to give people ownership over their data and create durable files that are compatible with older systems and other tools. The article argues that it's essential to create files that are readable for future generations and for your future self, even if it means moving away from apps and using plain text files instead.
