- Summary: Many people at OpenAI believe in building AGI, which drives them to scale large, costly AI models. This focus causes big environmental harm and exploits workers, while relying on uncertain hopes for huge future benefits. Instead, we should stop chasing AGI fantasies and use AI tools wisely for specific tasks with clear costs and benefits.
