## Highlights

- As a senior software engineer, your time and expertise are more valuable than ever. Saying no is not a sign of weakness or incompetence; it is an assertion of your boundaries and a demonstration of your ability to prioritize effectively. By declining tasks or requests that do not align with your current priorities or capacity, you create room for projects that truly matter and have the potential to yield significant results. This empowers you to focus on your core responsibilities, contribute meaningful work, and provide guidance to your team. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j70xc7tpteamym7p2qn9fh5r))
- If you find yourself frequently declining requests due to an overwhelming workload, consider redistributing responsibilities among team members. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j70xj7m07d7cz7yrz804xtcf))
- By leveraging the expertise and capabilities of your team, you can maintain productivity and deliver exceptional results. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j70xhzvpja1nvn3hkk47xc56))
