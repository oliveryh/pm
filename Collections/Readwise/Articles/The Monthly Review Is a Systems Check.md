- **☐ 5. Review Someday/Maybe items**
  The whole purpose of this category, as explained by GTD, is to be able to capture open loops that are spinning around your brain, but not necessarily commit to doing them immediately. It gives you the space to play with a wide diversity of possibilities, while focusing your attention on the ones that are “next.” ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gqw5kwq26xj4pr88ypsgqk50))
