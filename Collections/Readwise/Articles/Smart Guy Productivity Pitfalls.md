## Highlights

- **Commit to getting something done every day.** When you show up in the morning have a well defined set of things to finish that day. Stay as late as you have to in order to finish. By committing to finishing that task other distractions will naturally fall by the wayside. For example, I have jiu-jitsu training at 7pm. If I screw off too much during the day, I don't get to train. Also, by committing to a task, you avoid "being busy" instead of "getting work done", [they're not the same thing](http://bookofhook.blogspot.com/2013/01/being-busy-isnt-same-as-getting-work.html).
