- Category: #articles
- Summary: Transitioning from an engineer to a manager has been an interesting journey, filled with both positives and negatives. As a manager, the ability to impact the product and team is a significant advantage, offering more long-term career opportunities compared to staying in an engineering role. However, challenges like enforcing corporate decisions, managing social tensions, always being on call, and dealing with delayed feedback loops can make the role demanding and frustrating at times. Despite the drawbacks, moving into management can provide a fresh challenge for those seeking to broaden their skill set and perspective within the tech industry.
