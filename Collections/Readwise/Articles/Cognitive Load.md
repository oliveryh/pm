- Summary: Cognitive load refers to the mental effort required for developers to understand code, and high cognitive load can lead to confusion, wasting time and resources. It is important to minimize extraneous cognitive load by simplifying code structure and reducing unnecessary complexities. By doing so, we make it easier for developers to focus on the intrinsic challenges of their tasks.
