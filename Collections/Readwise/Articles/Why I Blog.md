## Highlights
- Writing is one of the best tools I have to clarify my own thinking. I'm a natural ruminator. I'm not good at thinking on the spot. I prefer to have time to think through details, connections, nuances, and consequences.
  Writing lets me do that while also helping me avoid going around in circles. When thoughts are in my head, it's easy for them to get jumbled up. I miss things, and I keep coming back to the same thoughts, leading to the unproductive ruminating.
