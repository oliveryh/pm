## Highlights

- Shipping products is hard. What makes it hard is that typical products involve multiple teams and multiple dependencies. Navigating these challenges is non-trivial. There are technical challenges to overcome, but those are typically not the biggest blockers. (There are enough smart people in the room to take care of that, unless its some greenfield domain like AR/VR/Self-Driving etc), ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gqqs9j3xp3jh9drdjefyy01b))
- You should arrive at a score that quantifies the benefits of prioritizing this task. Is it a highly requested feature ? Is it a competitive advantage ? Does it reduce cost ? Does it reduce the time to delivery ? ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gqqsd3pxy3qs1zjp1zgrgsfz))
