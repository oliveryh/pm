## Metadata

- Full Title: My productivity app is a never-ending .txt file
- Category: #articles
- Summary: The author explains their productivity system, which revolves around a text file that serves as a to-do list, research notebook, and meeting minutes all in one. They use an online calendar to keep track of future plans and schedule, and every night, they create a daily to-do list by taking all the items on their calendar for the next day and appending it to the text file. The daily list also doubles as a record of everything they have ever done, and they use various shorthands and tags to make it easily searchable. They also discuss their email workflow and how they handle incoming messages.
