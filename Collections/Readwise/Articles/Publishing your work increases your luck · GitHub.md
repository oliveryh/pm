## Highlights
- The goal is not to become famous, the goal is to increase the chances of luck finding us. For me, one of the most helpful ways to think about this has always been the concept of the “Luck Surface Area,” described in an [old post by Jason Roberts](https://www.codusoperandi.com/posts/increasing-your-luck-surface-area). ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kgpga21svecbs7cqstk56c1g))
- Watch the communities where you hang out and see what people are sharing and what gets noticed. Is it something you could have done? Is it something you’ve *already* done? At its worst this could lead you in the direction of becoming bitter, critical, and thinking that you’re smarter than everyone. To that I say “resist!” There is no life there. My encouragement to you is to view that as objective evidence that people want to know all of the things that you already know! There is a huge opportunity for you, should you decide to start sharing your work. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kgpgdw643wtqrfzgs76j7mm6))
