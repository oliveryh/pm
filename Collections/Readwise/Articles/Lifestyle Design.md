## Highlights
- Few truths are self-evident. To start from zero, choose independently-verifiable axioms. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d02n584dvdngkmme4kwen3))
- Two premises seem obvious and useful:
  • Premise 1: [suffering is bad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suffering-focused_ethics)
  • [**survive:**](https://taylor.town/constructive-lifestyle-design#survive) prevent yourself from immediate suffering
  • [**live:**](https://taylor.town/constructive-lifestyle-design#live) prevent yourself from future suffering
  • Premise 2: [consciousness exists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cogito,_ergo_sum); [entropy exists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laws_of_thermodynamics); attention is scarce
  • [**thrive:**](https://taylor.town/constructive-lifestyle-design#thrive) maximize attention ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d03d3njnacf2wpt8bqg4vw))
- Don’t suffer. Thirst, hunger, sickness, cold, fatigue, etc. are not mysterious forces. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d03zweyrwycqc18n8gbg56))
- Consensus favors the following advice:
  • drink a moderate amount of water
  • eat a moderate amount of nutritious foods
  • sleep plenty
  • regulate body temperature
  • exercise often ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d04yjps76my4jvd9pkbsph))
- To make living easy, align yourself with reality. Many problems are fabricated when your mind diverges from the world around you.
  You can independently verify your software bugs by studying your mind. [Your experience exists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cogito,_ergo_sum), but do you control it? Sit idly and focus on your breath – what can you observe about yourself?
  Your brain is plastic. Many errant thought patterns can be corrected through observation and practice:
  • mistaking sensory input for reality
  • maintaining unproductive emotions
  • obsessing over trifles
  • hallucinating facts
  • ignoring biases
  To supercharge self-observations, probe others. Engage in deep conversations.
  [Assume others exist.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solipsism) Pretend like everybody experiences your same range of pain and pleasure. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d05vzvy7cx2593pebcfj9p))
- Thrive
  Life is a [mapless territory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Map–territory_relation). You are a faulty compass.
  Many people peddle maps for the mapless territory. Few worldviews produce any consistent effect. Simple heuristics fail at this level. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d07r5eqbm3ec3t51668fqn))
- ttention & Happiness
  You are finite.
  To maximize your remaining experience, optimize the two largest levers in your mind: attention & happiness. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d08a1akgqvac6mcygg9r74))
- Attention
  Attention is the act of spending consciousness.
  But attention varies in quality. Observe your mind driving, sleeping, cooking, watching television, writing, doing math homework, etc. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d08wzta0yga44q9k6d48dw))
- Focus on fun. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01h3d09bj4m8p0w885jrvpt2xt))
