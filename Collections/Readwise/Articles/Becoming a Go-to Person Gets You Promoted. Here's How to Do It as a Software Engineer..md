- Summary: To get promoted as a software engineer, one strategy is to become a go-to person or an expert in a specific area. This could be a technical expertise, such as databases or Python, or a product domain expertise, like onboarding flows or payment portals. Being seen as a leader and having others seek out your advice is key. To find your expertise area, consider what you're good at, passionate about, and what has an impact on your team or company. Once you've identified your expertise area, it's important to be visible and add value. This can be done through activities like giving presentations, leading projects, answering questions, and solving unowned problems. Being known as an expert can lead to more opportunities and faster promotions.
