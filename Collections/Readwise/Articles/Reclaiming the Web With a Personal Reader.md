- Summary: The author of the article describes their experience with burnout and disillusionment with the software industry. They turned to Mastodon, an alternative to Twitter, and discovered a community of people seeking a more open and independent web. However, they found that Mastodon did not meet their needs as an information hub. They then decided to build their own personal feed reader, prioritizing ease of use and the user experience. They chose to use Python and created a classification system to sort the feed by frequency of updates, ensuring that they saw the least frequent sources first.
