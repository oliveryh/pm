## Highlights
- If we keep seeing only men as heads of corporations, it starts to seem ‘natural’ that only men should be heads of corporations
- But by far the worst thing we do to males – by making them feel they have to be hard – is that we leave them with very fragile egos. The harder a man feels compelled to be, the weaker his ego is.
  And then we do a much greater disservice to girls, because we raise them to cater to the fragile egos of males.
- Some people will say, ‘Well, poor men also have a hard time.’ And they do.
  But that is not what this conversation is about. Gender and class are different. Poor men still have the privileges of being men, even if they do not have the privileges of being wealthy.
