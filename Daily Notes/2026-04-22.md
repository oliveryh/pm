# Kraken Talk 

I have been working on registration this past week or so. 

Workshop

- Slide 1 - At first glance
	- New to a concept
	- At least one customer or specialist
	- Logging onto support site / consumer site
	- Trigger something, get information
- Slide 2 - Behind the curtain
	- Begin refining
		- Multiple different customer needs
		- Different territories
		- Configuration such as environment variables, feature flags, client variation
	- You have all sorts of business rules and conditions in your code
	- You may send these people an email, this is where you create a user, and this is where you show a warning to the customer
- Slide 3 - Understanding Complexity
	- Inherent or Incidental complexity
	- Opportunities to simplify
	- You could dog-food, clicking around the UI - great for beginners
	- Generate flow charts using LLMs or call graphs
	- The former is a great way to empathise with the customer but isn't scalabe
	- The latter is quite rigid and sometimes a little too technical
	- Why not both?
- Slide 4
	- Work with PMs and Designers
