- We can't care about something far away without a story. For example if we hear that there is a terrorist attack in Africa, we can't empathise because we don't understand them.
- There are only a limited number of archetypes of new stories
- Art is the discipline of framing news in a story that we care about. [^allan-de-botton]
- We are all implicated in the wrong-doings of the weakest in our societies

[^allan-de-botton]: [Alain De Botton on the news](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yKvOW6RwmFg)
