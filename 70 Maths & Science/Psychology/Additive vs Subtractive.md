Suppose you have a shape like one of the ones above. To complete a project, there are many different people involved.

![](https://cdn.sett.com/images/user/20121021/SixArtists8bpp2eed36e1871a56224de10a6259c8f914.png)

Now if someone sees you do some of the work in this circle, they can make the reasonable assumption you'll do the rest of the work in the circle. However generally our work is not additive. You can't simply do twice what you're already doing to satisfy the projects requirements (Do twice as many PR reviews).

Instead, creative work is iterative, convergent, and therefore subtractive. You identify what needs to be done and work until you have converged.
