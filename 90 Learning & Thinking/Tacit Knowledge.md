*Tacit Knowledge is knowledge that cannot be captured through words alone*

Tacit knowledge happens through instances were we might emulate someone until you internalise the principles behind the actions.

Natural Decision Making (NDM) is a means of studying how people perform cognitive complex functions in real-world situations.

Giving people a list of procedures to execute blindly denies them the possibility to build expertise. [^1]
