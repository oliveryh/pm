# Writing Flashcards

Answering a card should be single mental motion. [^gresearch]

Instead of the following:

Q. What is the composition of Air?

You might ask the following

Q. How many elements compose air?
Q. What is a mnemonic to remember the composition of air?
Q. What is the nth most popular element in air?

[^gresearch]: https://www.gresearch.co.uk/article/anki-as-learning-superpower-computer-science-edition/
