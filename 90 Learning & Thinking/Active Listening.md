# Active Listening

- Validate them (never make them wrong)
- Encourage them to keep talking (e.g. "Go on", "Tell me more")
	- This gives them a blank slate of conversation
- Ask exploratory questions
- Avoid why questions (Accusing)
- Grunt and nod to demonstrate listening
- Paraphrase what was said (do not repeat verbatim)
- Reflect back the impact on them and how they're feeling
