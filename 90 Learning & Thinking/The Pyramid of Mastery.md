- **Elements** - The fundamental concepts that define a domain
- **Rules** - The rules that govern the domain. Explain how elements interact with each other
- **Tools** - Defines the instruments you have at your disposal
- **Framework** - A system comprised of a subset of Elements, Rules, and Tools. They are an abstraction layer hiding what's beneath, focusing on a particular goal.

![[the-pyramid-of-mastery-1.png]]

An example of this in the context of cooking:

- Elements: Understanding proteins, carbohydrates, cutting techniques, etc.
- Rules: How proteins are affected when they're fried vs boiled. How emulsification works
- Tools: Knife, chopping board, blender.
