# Rules of Note Taking

- Store things that surprise you, not stuff you already know
- Store things related to projects, not trivia

> *"The way that I see it, everything I learn and jot down is something for me to act on at some point in my life. If I read something that I never thought would influence the way I lived, it wouldn’t have value to me, and I simply wouldn’t write it down."* - **TheSephist**

# Progressive Summarisation

*The process of taking things you learn and transferring it through time in its many forms*
