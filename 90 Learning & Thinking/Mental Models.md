Applying different mental models allows us to gain a richer understanding of the world around us.

# General Thinking Concepts

- **Probabilistic Thinking** - With infinitely complex sets of factors, probilistic thinking helps us identify the most likely outcomes and prepare accordingly.
- **Hanlon's Razor** - We should not attribute to malice that which is more easily explained by stupidity. Basically, giving others' the benefit of the doubt is more constructive.
- **Occam's Razor** - Simpler explanations are more likely to be true than complicated ones.
- **Inversion** - Think of the end goal and work backwards to determine what needs to be done
