# Lessons Learnt

- Don't forget to get travel insurance
- Make sure to pre-load all the necessary maps on GPS and phone
- Make sure you have the right cables for the devices you have
- Download offline maps
- Research things to do in the area
- Book any exclusive things ahead of time
- Soller Plaza will give us 10% discount if we book through them directly next time
- Make sure I can get back to Warwick at a reasonable time via public transport
- Bring antihistamines to countries with mosquitos
- Research before the trip
    - Where ATMs are
    - If cash is required on public transport / multi trip tickets are available
- Phone battery doesn't last very long when navigating
- Download things in the terminal
- Make sure to bring the original USB charger as ports that are available in the public are likely to be this one
