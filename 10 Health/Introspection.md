# Weaknesses

*The following describe behaviours that are seemingly benign, but have negative effects.*

- Staying later at work than expected on a regular basis → Ruins the balance I intend to have in life and offsets a timeline of routines that I generally adhere to.
- Letting chores build up rather than dealing with them on a daily basis → Puts myself in a bad environment in which I feel unmotivated to be productive.

# Satisfaction

## Work

It's clear that completing a small task early on, is a good way to start the day. I've noticed however that the following decrease my satisfaction of a task:

- A long time spent on the task without clear milestones (6 hours spent on a task)
- Many new tasks without some clear idea of their priorities or completion

I think in order to resolve this, I will need to have a clearer idea of steps and think more clearly about the entire workflow to get me to a goal. This worked well in Robert Chang's process of writing objectives clearly.

Additionally, ancillary tasks such as checking emails and having time to research should not be left by the way side. Deep mode is good but in the task that prompted me to write this, I was making stupid mistakes because I wasn't being careful about my process.

Finding how a learning opportunity can be integrated into the task at hand is also a good thing.
