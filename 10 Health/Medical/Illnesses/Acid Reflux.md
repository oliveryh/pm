## Symptom Spectrum

The spectrum illustrates the level of pain that are experienced.
- 0 - Absolutely no notice of this symptoms whatsoever
- 2 - Discomfort. Noticed sensations in the area but can easily be ignored
- 4 - Mild Pain. Sensations are noticed throught the day and there may be some mild pain
- 6 - Moderate Pain, interferes with regular activities
- 8 - Moderate Pain throughout the day. Can't be ignored, interferes with Concentration.
- 10 - Severe pain, requiring rest
