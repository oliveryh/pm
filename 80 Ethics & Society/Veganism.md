# How to Sustain a Vegan Diet

## Vegan Plate Model

- 50% Fruit and Vegetables
- 25% Wholegrains
    - Wholegrain Cereals
        - Rye
        - Oats
        - Wheat
        - Barley
        - Millet
        - Rice
    - Pseudo cereals
        - Quinoa
        - Buckwheat
- 25% Plant Based Protein
    - Pulses
        - Lentils
        - Peas
        - Beans
    - Cereals
    - Soy
        - Tofu
        - Tempeh
    - Nuts & Seeds
        - Walnuts
        - Almonds
        - Chia Seeds

## Required Nutrients

- B12
    - Fortified Milk and Cereals
    - Nutritional Yeast
- Iron
    - Fortified Milk and Cereals
    - Grains (Quinoa, Brown Rice, Oatmeal)
    - Vegetables (Leafy Greens)
- Vitamin D
