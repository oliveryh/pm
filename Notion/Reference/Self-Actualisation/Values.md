# My Values

## Essentialism

When a process needs better control, it is tempting to add complexity. I am easily overwhelmed by too many contexts, therefore simplicity is key. The term 'work smart, not hard' comes to mind.
- Using Money Dashboard to handle vacation payment handling
- Boiling down a photo collection just to those photos that make you truly happy, instead of adding complexity to the logistics of keeping everything

## Freedom

The freedom to never be held back by financials, physical ability, or mental barriers.
- Earning enough money to have unique experiences
- Investing time into my body to ensure I have the freedom to take part in adventures/sports
- Better confidence in the own abilities, will give me the freedom to take on new opportunities

## Variety

Incorporating variety into my activities, to ensure I don't get bored, but also diversify my understanding of the world.
- Trying out new meals when I cook
- Being open to new (and scary) experiences like dancing and singing

## Flexibility

Being in a situation where I can reduce inertia
- Anytime travel tickets
- Monthly rolling contract rent

## Opportunity

Creating moments which would not have otherwise existed. In the past I would wait for things to happen to me, however I have learnt now that, good things happen to those that go an do things.
- Organising TA Holiday
- Going to meet up with friends
- Encouraging others to engage in conversation, if they're not talkative

## Balance

I believe that all life categories should be tended to in some way or another. However I've already experienced that too much time at work, can cause me to neglect relationships and my physical health. Too much time spent focused on physical health may cause me to neglect relationships. Balance is key.
- Recording my time spend on life categories to ensure I'm getting a balance
- Setting out priorities and being more efficient, to ensure no time is lost

# End Goal

The main end goal is to have *freedom*. Freedom to go where I want without the burden of personal belongings. To speak multiple languages, have no physical barrier of taking part in something.

## A Future Oliver

- 100% Vegan
- Unreserved, not being the life of the party, but someone who is invested in conversations and social situations
- A steward to earth and to animals
    - Stepping up for animal rights
    - Demonstrating reasonably achievable actions to reduce impact
- Physically fit
- Strong time investment with few important people in my life
