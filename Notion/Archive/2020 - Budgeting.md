## Key Factors
We can therefore deduce the key factors here
- CS has a very good pension scheme of 13% matching which substantially adds to wealth over time
- Not working from May until September may be too damaging financially, so another option would be to seek out part time work at CS until the summer and then quit
- Changing job without thinking about pension etc, could result in loss of £40k over the course of three years.
- Changing job at the moment isn't easy, so it may be better to keep steady income, focus on my application to universities and then seek out work if I am not successful with my application.
