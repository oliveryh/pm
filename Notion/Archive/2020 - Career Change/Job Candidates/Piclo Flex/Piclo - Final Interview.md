## Requirements Gathering

**Types of Requirement**
- Functional Requirement
    - Relate to the product capabilities, usability, features, etc
- Non-Functional Requirements
    - Anything not related to a products functionality
    - Performance, stability, technical specifications, security


**Scope Creep**
- Have a clear outline of requirements and why you may or may not do something
- This can be used with the client to avoid scope creep and assure them you're staying within budget


**Process**
1. Create notes of meetings with clients
2. Send out minutes (actions, red flags etc) with internal team and then once confirmed, send out to client
3. Send on to client to have consensus
4. Understand if any style-guide is to be used
5. Create wire-frame or annotated version of the page
6. List out functional requirements in relation to each annotated section
7. Break functional requirements into tasks
- Adopts a "shared-nothing" component-based architecture
