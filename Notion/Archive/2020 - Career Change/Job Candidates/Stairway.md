# Role Description

## Description

Stairway is a digital personal tutor for STEM subjects 🎓
We are on a mission to enable anyone to have access to effective and enjoyable education.📈
Stairway was founded by recent students, who are extremely focused on creating a great product to address the specific challenges faced by students
today, through a combination of a great product and content 💻
We are looking for a Junior Full Stack Developer to help us scale our existing web application to students around the world!
You'll be a valued member of the Product team, with a large amount of input
into ideating and planning new features, along with the technical
infrastructure to support a fast-growing consumer product 🎓

### Requirements

- Experience with Python, Flask and React.js (at least two of three)
- Experience working with NoSQL databases
- Excellent communication and ability to work as part of a team
- Experience working alongside a design team to implement design specifications using HTML, SCSS and React.js
