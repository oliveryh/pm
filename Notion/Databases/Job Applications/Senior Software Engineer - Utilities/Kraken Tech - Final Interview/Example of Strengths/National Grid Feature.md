# Context
- We had recently partnered with National Grid to contribute to the Local Constraint Market
- Our product would be in the control room
- We knew that the use of the feature would be in a high stress environment, interacting with it a short notice
- This was a pilot, so high stakes

# Action
- We had some interviews with the training lead of the product to understand how her team are using the feature and what additional items they need
- For example they used the feature in half a screen frequently
- We had a captive audience who were ready to give feedback and this meant we had a really short feedback loop

# Result
- The feature did needed very little amendments and is still used
- We were able to add some QOL additions that reduced the chances of errors thanks to early feedback

# Learning
- Build the right stuff for the customer at the right time
