# Context
- Recently become engineering lead of my team
- We were working on expanding a core part of our marketplace (the matching engine) to a new region
- The CTO was concerned that a considerable amount of work wasn’t being acknowledged or planned for

# Action
- I spoke with our Product Manager and raised the risk that our current roadmap would miss this dependency
- We put together an improved process to surface larger technical risks and discuss these sooner
- I spoke with my LM how I could have done a better job at communicating

# Result
- The tech requirements were given time on our roadmap
- I was able to use this example to quickly learn about expectations about my role

# Learnings
- Communicate early - culture of trust
- To take conflict and use it as a learning opportunity
- I needed to take ownership over parts of the future roadmap
